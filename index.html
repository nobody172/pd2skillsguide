Skills are the bread and butter of Payday 2. They have the largest impact on the strength of a loadout, and can easily be the difference between a successful heist and a failed one. There are 90 skills, and each can be "aced" for an additional effect which brings the total number of unique skills to 180

Perk decks are selectable sets "decks" similar to skills that are integral to Payday 2 builds. There are 23 perk decks in Payday 2, all with different purposes, and some more effective than others.

Payday 2 has many hidden (and not so hidden) mechanics that interact with the Skill system in various ways
